About Me

I'm passionate about creating interactive data visualizations that make complex information accessible and engaging. My work focuses on transforming data into compelling visual narratives that help people understand relationships, patterns, and insights.

With expertise in D3.js and modern web technologies, I specialize in building interactive visualizations that combine technical precision with intuitive design. My recent work on programming language genealogy demonstrates my ability to research complex topics and present them through innovative visual interfaces.

I'm always exploring new ways to visualize data and push the boundaries of what's possible with web-based interactive graphics. Whether it's historical timelines, network relationships, or statistical analysis, I love finding the right visual approach for each unique dataset.
